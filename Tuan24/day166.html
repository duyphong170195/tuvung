Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Nghĩa Ví dụ
precisely /prəˈsaɪs.li/ (adv) exactly; used to emphasize the accuracy of what you are saying
  • We arrived at the hotel at precisely 10:30.
  • That's precisely what I meant.
  • The fireworks begin at eight o'clock precisely.
  • She’s precisely the kind of person we’re looking for.
  • (idiom) more precisely: used to show that you are giving more detailed and accurate information about something you have just mentioned
  • The problem is due to discipline, or, more precisely, the lack of discipline, in schools.
    		•
    railyard (n) an area having a network of railway tracks and sidings for storage and maintenance of cars and engines
    donation /doʊˈneɪ.ʃən/ (n) từ việc ủng hộ, quyên góp; việc quyên góp, ủng hộ tiền
    (money or goods that are given to a person or an organization in order to help them, or the act of giving them)
  • Would you like to make a donation (=give money) to our charity appeal?
    		•
    reflection /rɪˈflek.ʃən/ (n) ảnh phản chiếu (an image that you can see in a mirror, glass, water or on any reflective surface)
  • She was looking at her reflection in the mirror.
    		•
    slightly /ˈslaɪt.li/ (adj) 1 chút (a little)
  • She's slightly taller than her sister.
  • January’s sales were slightly better than average.
  • He was someone I already knew slightly.
  • (adj) slight: small in amount or degree
  • a slight improvement
  • a slight change of plan
  • He's got a headache and a slight fever.
    		•
