    Man: So Melanie, do you come from a big family?

    Woman: I come from a medium-sized family. I have a mom, a dad, and then one brother and one sister.

    Man: I see.

    Woman: Yeah, but I am the oldest of the siblings in my family. So, it's kind of funny. I feel like growing up, my mom and dad were much stricter with me than my brothers and sisters.

    Man: Yeah, that tends to happen.

    Woman: Yeah, it's not really fair, but it does tend to happen so I feel like growing up, I had the earlier curfews and everything was being checked. I was being checked up on more. But yeah, I mean...

    Man: I guess your brother and sister got away with murder.

    Woman: They got away with murder for sure. So what about you? Do you have any brothers and sisters?

    Man: Actually, I'm from a strict nuclear family - mother, father, my older sister (she's my only sibling) and myself.

    Woman: Oh, nice.

    Man: Just a normal family, but I wonder, do you take after someone in your family?

    Woman: Well, I think looks-wise not really, but personality-wise I think a little bit of both - my mom and my dad. I think with my mom, I am sensitive and a little more emotional like my mom, and then with my dad, he's a great storyteller and I feel like I also like to talk and tell stories, but sometimes I might talk too much. To the point where my husband says, "Come on, get to the point." So yeah, but I think I get that from my mom and my dad. Do you have, by any chance, like a large extended family that you're close to?

    Man: Oh, yeah. We have a huge extended family that I'm very close to.

    Woman: Okay.

    Man: My mom is the oldest of eight.

    Woman: Oh, wow. I can totally relate.

    Man: So family reunions are huge. When I go there, there's 20-25 cousins. It's a lot of fun.

    Woman: It's fun. But yeah it's a lot of people, I can relate because, my dad is also one of 10 brothers and sisters. So we also have some pretty fun family reunions.

    Man: Okay.

    Woman: In fact, we're having one this summer.

    Man: Really?

    Woman: For my brother's wedding. So I'm excited.

    Man: Yeah, I hope you have a lot of fun with that.

    Woman: Thanks!
